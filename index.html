Attention:

The sequence alignment results might not always be correct, especially with sequences that have repeating parts (like ACGAA). However, the substitution matrix is accurate and can still help check the alignment results.

This tool is not ideal for very long sequences or those with big length differences. It works best for short sequences and is especially helpful for class tasks.
